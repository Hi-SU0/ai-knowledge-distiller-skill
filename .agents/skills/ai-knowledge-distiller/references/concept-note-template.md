## Field and merge rules

- Use the most stable standard term as the filename and heading.
- Put Chinese names, English names, abbreviations, historical terms, and common spelling variants in `aliases`.
- Before creating the file, search filenames, headings, aliases, and existing wikilinks.
- Preserve each source's explanation under its own subsection. Never blend multiple sources into an unattributed statement.
- Put only supported overlap in `多个来源的共识`; record disagreements with source links.
- Mark external additions and stale claims explicitly.
